BATTLESHIPS

A GAME BY PAVEL POPOV


THE RULES ARE SIMPLE: FIRST PLACE YOUR SHIPS ON YOUR PLAYING FIELD BY CLICKING
ON THEIR NAMES AND THEN CLICKING ON THE CHOSEN TILE.
AN AIRCRAFT CARRIER TAKES UP 5 TILES, BATTLECRUISERS TAKE UP 3 AND A BOAT - ONLY 1.
AFTER YOU'VE PLACED YOUR SHIPS, IT'S TIME FOR THE GAME TO START!
CLICK ON THE ENEMY'S TILES TO SHOOT AT THEM!
GREEN MEANS YOU'VE SUCCESSFULLY HIT AN ENEMY SHIP.
TO MAKE THINGS MORE INTERESTING, YOU HAVE TWO BONUSES AT HAND:
A VOLLEY WILL CLEAR A WHOLE ROW OF THE ENEMY'S BOARD
A CLUSTER BOMB WILL CLEAR A 3X3 SQUARE OF THE ENEMY'S BOARD AROUND THE TILE YOU CLICKED
BUT BE AWARE, THE AI ALSO HAS ACCESS TO THESE BONUSES AND HE IS TRIGGER HAPPY!
I HOPE YOU HAVE AS MUCH FUN PLAYING IT AS I HAD CODING IT

ENJOY!
